RESOURCES:
For your convenience, we've compiled a few trusted sources of eye care information to make it easy to find exactly what you're looking for. Click on the logo to go to their site.

EYE SMART - This site features eye health information directly from the American Academy of Ophthalmology. Find details on diseases/conditions, symptoms, and vision correction methods. They also supply a wide range of tips and suggestions for everyday eye care and provide the latest in eye health news.

WEB MD - This site provides you with important health information, tools for managing your health, and support to people looking for information. They cover every aspect of healthcare, including a great selection of ocular health information.

ALL ABOUT VISION - This site is devoted to eye care education and features a breakdown of topics for different age groups, with lots of information for children. Here, you'll find downloadable eye-related infographics along with a vast collection of other helpful tools and information.
